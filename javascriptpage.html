Week 4 & 5: JavaScript!

Week 4 & 5 we were learning JavaScript. This week was quite the challenge, but I felt I had my biggest breakthrough at the end of the assignment. During this assignment, I was scouring stack overflow, W3 schools, emailing my professor, and even asking for tips from a friend who is a professional coder. I can tell he wanted me to struggle a little bit and did not just give up the answer. Finally it all clicked! I was quite proud that I was able to get my functions to work.
